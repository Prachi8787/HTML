Attractions or Activities

Paris

  • Eiffel Tower: Iconic landmark offering breathtaking views.
  • Louvre Museum: World's largest art museum, home to the Mona Lisa.
  • Notre-Dame Cathedral: Gothic architecture with stunning interiors.
  • Seine River Cruise: Scenic boat ride with views of Paris landmarks.
  • Montmartre: Artistic neighborhood with Sacré-Cœur Basilica.


Spain

  • La Sagrada Familia (Barcelona): Gaudi’s famous unfinished basilica.
  • Alhambra (Granada): Moorish palace and fortress complex.
  • La Rambla (Barcelona): Lively street for shopping and dining.
  • Running of the Bulls (Pamplona): Famous annual event during San Fermin Festival.


Las Vegas

  • Bellagio Fountains: Iconic water show in front of the Bellagio Hotel.
  • The Strip: Famous for casinos, hotels, and entertainment.
  • Cirque du Soleil Shows: World-renowned acrobatic performances.
  • Fremont Street: Pedestrian mall with light shows and attractions.
  • Hoover Dam: Impressive engineering marvel just outside the city.


Switzerland

  • The Matterhorn: Iconic mountain for hiking and skiing.
  • Lake Geneva: Beautiful lake with cruises and watersports.
  • Jungfraujoch: "Top of Europe" with stunning alpine views.
  • Château de Chillon: Medieval castle on Lake Geneva.
  • Lucerne: Picturesque city with medieval architecture and lake views.


Sydney

  • Sydney Opera House: Architectural wonder and cultural venue.
  • Sydney Harbour Bridge: Climb for panoramic city views.
  • Bondi Beach: Famous for surfing and coastal walks.
  • Taronga Zoo: Wildlife experience with views of Sydney Harbour.
